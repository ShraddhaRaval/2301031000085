Mindfulness Meditation

Mindfulness meditation involves paying attention to the present moment with openness, curiosity, and acceptance. It helps to reduce stress and improve mental clarity.

To practice mindfulness, find a quiet space, sit comfortably, and focus on your breath. Observe your thoughts without judgment, returning your focus to the breath when distracted.
